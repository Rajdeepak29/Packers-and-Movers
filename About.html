ABOUT US

SRD-Packers and Movers started off as a platform to address inefficiencies in the last mile logistics sector and transform the way goods are transported around cities, enabling lakhs of businesses move anything on-demand. We’ve grown manyfold since then, positively impacting the productivity of businesses, creating tremendous value for our partner-drivers and delivering happiness to a growing list of five cities. But our journey has just begun, our goal is to to deliver the world’s best end-to-end logistics platform and revolutionise the transport logistics sector.

Porter is strongly committed towards improving the quality of life of our partner drivers. Our partners enjoy flexible working hours, increased earnings and an effective work life balance alongwith additional benefits like discounted insurance and fuel costs. We’ve also taken up a lot of community development initiatives like higher education grants and scholarships for the children of our partners, dedicated health care assistance and vehicle leasing assistance.
